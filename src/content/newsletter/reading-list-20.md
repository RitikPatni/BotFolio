Hello Everyone,

I hope you have been doing great and are keeping yourself hydrated in this extreme summer.

## 📚 To Read

[Why did you originally build CSS-Tricks?](https://chriscoyier.net/2022/05/12/why-did-you-originally-build-css-tricks%ef%bf%bc/?utm_source=ritik&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=reading-list-20)

A truly inspiring story of how one of the best CSS blog site originated.

[Who Is Inclusive Design For?](https://heydonworks.com/article/who-is-inclusive-design-for/?utm_source=ritik&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=reading-list-20)

What people will be benefitted from you design your stuff with keeping inclusivity in mind

[Listen To Me And Not Google](https://heydonworks.com/article/listen-to-me-not-google/?utm_source=ritik&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=reading-list-20)

Google (and other big tech companies) has a really great group of engineers, but that doesn't mean that what they built is perfect and saying But Google/Microsoft/Amazon/etc. does it is never a good argument about anything.

[Test in many browsers](https://hidde.blog/test-in-many-browsers/?utm_source=ritik&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=reading-list-20)

This is something I can't stress enough upon. Try your websites in as many browsers as possible. I hate when a website breaks in a browser I prefer to use or see a dialog this website will not work in this browser, please use Chrome.

[Changing with the times](https://chrisburnell.com/article/changing-with-the-times/?utm_source=ritik&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=reading-list-20)

So many good tips about how you can blog things and what tools you can rely upon

[Unblocking Your Writing Blocks, Part 2: I’m Not an Expert nor a “Thought Leader”](https://matthiasott.com/notes/unblocking-your-writing-blocks-part-2-im-not-an-expert?utm_source=ritik&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=reading-list-20)

You might think it isn’t worth sharing, but maybe you also underestimate how much you actually know and overestimate how much others know?

[Divs are bad!](https://www.matuzo.at/blog/2022/divs-are-bad/?utm_source=ritik&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=reading-list-20)

…when you don’t use them correctly.

[Stop pushing the web forward](https://www.quirksmode.org/blog/archives/2015/07/stop_pushing_th.html?utm_source=ritik&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=reading-list-20)

I agree with some points made in this and disagree with some, but this is a pretty interesting viewpoint. One I hope you'll love to read.
