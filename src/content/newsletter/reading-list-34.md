Outline is a very helpful and useful property in CSS and yet, every now and then we come across scenarios where there is no outline and when you try to focus using the keyboard you will have no idea what are you focusing on. Read on here to find out why it is useful and how you can style it so that the next time someone asks you to remove the outline, you can tell them the uses and not blindly remove it.
