Hope everyone had an outstanding week. Mine was filled with interviews as I left my old job and transitioned into a new one. With that update, let's start with this week's interesting stuff…

## 📚 To Read

[The Grug Brained Developer](https://grugbrain.dev/)

A layman's guide to thinking like the self-aware smol brained

[What is Spoon Theory?](https://happiful.com/what-is-the-spoon-theory/)

Learned something new and insightful on mental health

[The Magic Cookie: How Lou Montulli cured the Web’s amnesia](https://hiddenheroes.netguru.com/lou-montulli)

More than two decades ago, Web pioneer Lou Montulli invented browser software cookies with the goal of preserving user privacy online. But then things took an unexpected turn.

[Help Me Help You: A Guide to Asking for Help](https://kittygiraudel.com/2022/07/15/a-guide-to-asking-for-help/)

An excellent article on how to ask for help. So many nice tips and suggestions so that neither you nor the maintainers/community-folks lose their mind.

[It’s 1997 and you want to build a website](https://thehistoryoftheweb.com/postscript/its-1997-and-you-want-to-build-a-website/)

How do you think web developers used to build websites before google became a thing where forums, support groups, communities is still way beyond the dreams. This one is an interesting read about those times, what it meant to build a website in that time.

[Moose facts](https://dev.to/cassidoo/moose-facts-1l9j)

Believe me, you intend to read these moose facts.
