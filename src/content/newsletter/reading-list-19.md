Hello Everyone,

Another interesting week for me. This week I've been reading up on [IndieWeb](https://indieweb.org) and have been experimenting with this. This seems extremely exciting stuff, and I'll probably have something to show in coming weeks. With this all, let's start with this week's reading list.

## 📚 To Read

[Why blog?](https://cagrimmett.com/thoughts/2022/04/26/why-blog/)

So many great reasons on why you should be blogging and why it should ideally be on your platform.

[Garbage Heap](https://blog.cjeller.site/garbage-heap)

I think I tend to reach that place pretty quickly with all the note-taking systems I start to use. I really want to have a nice system, but within a few days all I have is a Garbage Heap.

[My 2022 predictions from 2012](https://shkspr.mobi/blog/2022/05/my-2022-predictions-from-2012/)

I love reading this prediction blogs from someone's past. If you're interested in reading those, then I really loved this one.

[You Don’t Need A UI Framework](https://www.smashingmagazine.com/2022/05/you-dont-need-ui-framework/)

[Josh](https://www.joshwcomeau.com) is spot on with this one. Highly agree with every bit of what he said here.

One argument to end them all. Sarcasm Inside.
